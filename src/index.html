Veggie Pizza Delight

15 Min

•

Italian

•

1345 Cal

•

12 Ingredients

Indulge in the rich flavors of this ultimate Veggie Pizza Delight, loaded with black olives, jalapeños, cherry tomatoes, fresh basil, onions, and a generous amount of gooey cheese. Every bite offers a perfect balance of tangy, spicy, and cheesy goodness.

Ingredients

Pizza Dough

1 medium-sized dough ball

Tomato Sauce

1/2 cup (for spreading)

Mozzarella Cheese

2 cups, shredded

Cheddar Cheese

1 cup, shredded

Black Olives

1/4 cup, sliced

Jalapenos

1/4 cup, sliced

Cherry Tomatoes

1/2 cup, halved

Tomato

1 medium, sliced

Onion

1/2 medium, thinly sliced

Mushrooms

1/2 cup, sliced

Basil Leaves

A handful, fresh

Olive Oil

1 tablespoon
